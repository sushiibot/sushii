## features

<CardGrid>
  <Card title="Smart Moderation Cases" icon="document">
    Intelligent case management that tracks all mod actions whether done via
    commands or Discord's built-in tools. Never lose track of moderation history
    again.
  </Card>
  <Card title="XP and Levels" icon="star">
    Activity-based progression with automatic role rewards at milestones.
    Encourage your members to chat with rankings and leaderboards.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Message & Member Logging" icon="list-format">
    Track message edits and deletes, who reacted first, moderator actions, and
    member changes. Complete accountability for your server.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Emoji & Sticker Statistics" icon="bars">
    Detailed emoji and sticker usage stats to understand community engagement.
    See what content resonates with your members.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Custom Commands (Tags)" icon="comment">
    Create custom commands for FAQs, rules, or community info. Give your members
    quick access to important information.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Role Menus" icon="setting">
    Self-service role selection with buttons or dropdowns for colors, pronouns,
    notifications, and more. Let members customize their experience.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Giveaways" icon="random">
    Run engaging giveaways with requirements like roles, levels, or booster
    status. Build excitement and reward your community.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Utilities & Social" icon="puzzle">
    Essential utilities like reminders and keyword notifications, plus
    entertaining social features like reputation points and fishies.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>
